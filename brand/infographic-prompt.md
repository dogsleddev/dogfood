Create a tall vertical infographic poster (portrait, 4:5) for an AI-native
financial-planning (FP&A) software product called "Dogfood."

AESTHETIC: warm, editorial, premium — a beautifully designed outdoor-expedition
trail map crossed with a modern fintech brand. Flat vector illustration with a
subtle paper-grain texture, soft long shadows, and generous negative space.
Not photorealistic, not a screenshot, not cluttered.

COLOR PALETTE: warm cream / parchment background (#FAF7F1); deep midnight navy
(#0E1726) for anchor shapes and the title; terracotta-ember orange (#EC6D3F) as
the primary accent; warm amber-gold (#E7B23A) and calm sage green (#46C99A) as
secondary accents. Faint, very-low-contrast topographic contour lines in the
background.

TYPOGRAPHY: an elegant high-contrast serif (Fraunces style) for the large display
title, paired with a clean geometric sans (Geist style) for labels. Crisp,
legible, well-kerned, perfectly spelled.

CENTRAL CONCEPT: a winding dashed ember-colored TRAIL ROUTE (like a dogsled/hiking
route on a map) that climbs from the bottom of the poster up to a glowing NORTH
STAR at the top. Five small circular "station" medallions sit along the trail,
each with a simple line icon — the journey from raw data up to a confident
decision. A small four-point ember SPARK character (the AI guide, "Scout") rests
on the trail near the top with a soft glow.

HEADER (top): the name "Dogfood" in large serif; a small logo of three ascending
chevron arrows climbing left-to-right with the top chevron in ember; beneath it
the line "AI-native FP&A platform." The glowing north star sits above with the
tagline "Know your next move."

THE FIVE TRAIL STATIONS, bottom → top (use these exact captions):
1. "Closed actuals" — icon: a small base-camp tent / stacked ledger lines
2. "A living forecast" — icon: a rising trend line
3. "Scenarios in minutes" — icon: branching trail paths
4. "Statements that tie out" — icon: a balanced scale
5. "Decisions" — icon: a flag planted on a summit

FOOTER: small text "dogfood.cafe."

FEEL: confident, calm, premium, a little adventurous. Balanced composition, lots
of breathing room, muted warm lighting. Keep all text minimal, large, and
perfectly spelled.

## Format swaps
- **Slides (16:9 landscape):** change "tall vertical portrait 4:5" → "wide landscape 16:9", and "climbs from bottom to top" → "travels left to right."
- **Social (square):** request "1:1 square," keep the climb but compress to 3–4 stations.
- **Plainer look (no trail metaphor):** replace CENTRAL CONCEPT with "a clean vertical stack of five labeled cards, each with an icon, connected by a thin ember line."
